## First steps

New to SPU? This is the Beginner’s Guide.

If you don’t know anything about SPU, please check [What is SPU?](introduction.mdx#what-is-spu) first.

After that, follow [Installation Guidelines](install.mdx#installation-guidelines) to install SPU and [SPU Quickstart](../tutorials/quick_start.mdx) to go through a simple example.

Finally, Check other examples in [Tutorials](../tutorials/index.mdx).
